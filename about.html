Our Story

At Penielfield Secondary School, we are committed to providing a comprehensive education that nurtures academic excellence, creativity, and character development. Our mission is to prepare students for success in higher education and life beyond school.

Our Mission

To deliver well-rounded qualitative education, with good character, a high sense of self-discipline, dedicated service to humanity, and global integration through shared commitments.

Our Aim

is to nurture GLOBAL CHILDREN , believing that if they are BETTER adults, they will fit in globally. Academics without high potential, attributes, and boldness to tackle the world's challenges amount to nothing. Succinctly, our focus is that a child's education must be qualitative enough to build the skills to excel in life's real feat. Our children must be knowledgeable and have a feat of strength, civility, and fearless heart to navigate the challenges and trials of life.

Core Values

At Penielfield, we do not operate by rigid rules, but we uphold a strong code of conduct that guides respect for others and excellence in all we do. Our core values are built upon the following:

  • Self-esteem
  • Integrity
  • Discipline
  • Excellence
  • Creativity
  • Obedience
  • Shared Commitments
  • Team Spirit
