Secondary School

Shaping Future Leaders with Excellence and Integrity. At Penielfield Private Schools, our Secondary School is where potential meets purpose. From Junior to Senior Secondary, we provide a globally integrated curriculum that prepares students for academic success, leadership, and impact in a rapidly changing world.

What Makes Our Secondary School Exceptional

  • 📘 Blended curriculum combining Nigerian, British, and American standards for global competitiveness
  • 🧪 Advanced subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Literature, Economics, Government, and Further Math
  • 📝 Exam readiness for WAEC, NECO, IGCSE, SAT, TOEFL, and other international assessments
  • 🗣 Leadership development through student council, prefectship, public speaking, and mentorship programs
  • 🧠 Critical thinking and research skills embedded across subjects and projects
  • 🧩 Clubs and competitions: Robotics, Model UN, Creative Writing, Entrepreneurship, Debate, and STEM
  • 🎭 Creative expression through drama, music, visual arts, and media
  • 🧘 Pastoral care and counseling to support emotional well-being and personal growth
  • 🌍 Global perspective preparing students for international universities and careers
  • 🧼 Safe, inclusive, and tech-enabled learning spaces fostering innovation and collaboration

Secondary School – FAQs

Q1: What exams do students prepare for?

Our students are fully prepared for WAEC, NECO, IGCSE, SAT, TOEFL, and other international assessments, ensuring they are globally competitive.

Q2: What curriculum is used?

We deliver a blended curriculum that integrates Nigerian, British, and American standards, giving learners a strong foundation for higher education both locally and abroad.

Q3: Are leadership opportunities available?

Yes! Students can take on leadership roles as prefects, join the student council, and participate in enriching programs such as Model UN, debate, and entrepreneurship initiatives.

Q4: How do you support emotional well-being?

We provide pastoral care, counseling services, and mentorship programs to help students navigate adolescence with confidence and resilience.

Q5: Are students prepared for university admissions?

Absolutely. We offer career guidance, university prep workshops, and personalized support for both local and international applications.
