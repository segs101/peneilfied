Creche

A Gentle Beginning for Lifelong Learning. At Penielfield Private Schools, our Creche offers more than just childcare—it’s a nurturing launchpad for your child’s growth and happiness. We provide a warm, secure, and stimulating environment where love, learning, and laughter fill every day.

What Makes Our Creche Exceptional

  • 👶 Age-appropriate care for infants from 3 months, tailored to individual needs and rhythms
  • 🧸 Structured daily routines that build emotional security and a sense of belonging
  • 🎵 Sensory-rich activities including music, movement, and story time
  • 🛏 Comfortable sleep areas with soft lighting and child-safe furnishings
  • 🧼 Hygienic, secure facilities meeting the highest safety standards
  • 📋 Daily progress reports and milestone tracking for parents
  • 💬 Open communication with caregivers for peace of mind
  • 👫 Gentle socialization to help children build trust and interact with peers
  • 🧠 Early stimulation programs supporting cognitive, motor, and emotional development
  • 🕒 Flexible scheduling options for working families

Creche – FAQs

Q1: What age can my baby start at the Creche?

We accept infants from 3 months old, once they’ve received essential immunizations and are ready for group care.

Q2: What kind of care do you provide for infants?

Our caregivers offer personalized attention, gentle routines, and sensory-rich activities in a safe, hygienic, and emotionally secure environment.

Q3: Will I receive updates about my child’s day?

Yes! We provide daily reports, milestone tracking, and open communication to keep you informed and involved.

Q4: Are meals provided?

We support both parent-provided meals and school-prepared options, depending on your preference. All feeding follows strict hygiene and safety protocols.

Q5: Can I visit or check in during the day?

Absolutely. We encourage scheduled visits and maintain an open-door policy for emergencies or concerns.
