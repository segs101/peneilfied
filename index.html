📚 Our Curriculum

At Penielfield Private Schools (PPS), we believe every child is unique and capable of achieving their personal best. Our curriculum nurtures academic excellence, creativity, character, and global readiness—from the earliest years to secondary school.

🧒 Early Years Foundation

We combine modern teaching methods with first-class facilities to help every child flourish. Focus areas include language, numeracy, creativity, and social development.

📘 Primary School

Our blended curriculum (Nigerian, British & American) builds strong foundations in English, Mathematics, Science, and the Arts, while encouraging creativity and expression.

🎓 Secondary School

Advanced studies prepare students for higher education, leadership, and global opportunities with emphasis on critical thinking and problem-solving.
